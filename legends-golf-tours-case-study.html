Website

A modern logo for a modern golf brand

Legends Golf Tours wanted to offer something unheard of - luxury trips with golfing legends, revisiting their greatest moments and spending plenty of time together on and off the course. They needed a logo that broke away from heritage brands, while still being golf-related.

A polished website builds trust in the product

Each tour has a daily itinerary, with an animated progress bar to keep users scrolling down to the booking section.

Navigation links are hidden when scrolling down, and show when scrolling up, so users only see content relevant to them in that moment.

The navbar has a dynamic CTA button which is relevant to the current page, e.g. 'Book this tour' on tour pages.

An intuitive & flexible CMS saves both time and money

Using Webflow's CMS allowed the client to update information on a daily basis without needing to contact me.

Instead of relying on a designer or developer to make routine edits, they can log in through a simple editor interface and add new tours, change prices, or update itineraries instantly.

With the ability to preview pages and edit the content directly on the page, the process is extremely intuitive.
